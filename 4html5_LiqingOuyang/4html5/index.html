RONGHAO LI
李荣浩

Ronghao Li (born July 11, 1985) is a Chinese musician, singer-songwriter and producer. He has released eight studio albums and won the Golden Melody Award for Best New Singer in 2013. He has held four major concert tours and became the second singer from the Chinese mainland to hold concerts at Hong Kong Coliseum and Taipei Arena. In 2020, he was ranked 59th on the Forbes China Celebrity 100 List.
